Server Rules

and punishments

1. No Explicit or Inappropriate Content

  • No NSFW builds, signs, books, or skins.
  • No gore, shock content, or disturbing materials.

Punishment: Immediate warn or ban, depending on severity.

2. No Spamming

  • No spam in chat (e.g. repeated messages or characters, even if it's a single word).

Punishment: Warn or Mute

3. Use Respectful Language

  • Swearing is allowed within reason — excessive profanity = mute/warn.
  • Racial slurs = instant ban (length determined by staff).
  • Hate speech = warn or ban, depending on severity.
  • Targeted harassment or toxic behavior = mute and/or warn.

4. Respect Other Players

  • No griefing or trolling outside of declared wars/conflict.
  • No bypassing war rules for harassment.
  • No spawn killing.
  • No naked killing.

Punishment: Warn or ban, depending on severity.

5. Sensitive Topics

  • Avoid trolling with sensitive topics like race, religion, or war crimes.

Punishment: Warn, mute, or ban depending on content and intent.

6. Use Systems Properly

  • Do not exploit bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics.

Punishment: Warn or ban

7. No Hacking or Cheating

  • No use of hack clients, mods, resourcepacks, etc (e.g. kill aura, fly, x-ray, baritone, etc.).
  • Bypass cheats are still considered hacking.
  • Any item/block/money duplication is considered cheating.

Punishment: Instant ban, length depends on severity.
