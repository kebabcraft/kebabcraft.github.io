Worlds

How our world system works

    We have 3 types of worlds

  • Earth Type

    This type of world is a 1:326 replica of the real life Earth

  • Minecraft Type

    This type of world is a normal minecraft world

  • Lobby Type

    You can access all types of worlds from here

    • And we have 4 Worlds:

  • Earth World

    This World is earth type, you can claim here, and this is the main part of the server.

  • Farmworld

    This World is meant for farming resources, so that the earth world doesn't suffer from gigantic holes and stuff like that.

    This World is Minecraft Type.

  • Farmsworld

    You can build your ugly farms in this world, so that you dont have to look at them in the Earth World.

    This World is Minecraft Type.

  • Lobby

    You can access all Worlds from here and you can access this world from all worlds with /lobby. A peaceful nice hangout spot with a very legal cartel.

    This World is Lobby Type.
